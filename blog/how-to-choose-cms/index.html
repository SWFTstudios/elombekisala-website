How to choose a CMS for your site

February 22, 2025 · 5 min read

Choice depends on what you’re publishing: marketing pages and blog → Webflow CMS is simple and visual. Products and store → Shopify. Need a custom front with an API? Consider a headless CMS (Contentful, Sanity, etc.) with a static or app front end.

Webflow CMS

Best when you want design and content in one place. Great for portfolios, blogs, and team pages. Limited if you need complex e-commerce or many integrations.

Shopify

Built for products and checkout. Use it when the primary goal is selling. Pair with Webflow or a custom front if you need a unique storefront.

Headless

When you need maximum flexibility and have dev resources. Content lives in the CMS; the site or app fetches via API.
