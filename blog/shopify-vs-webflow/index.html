Shopify vs Webflow for your storefront

February 1, 2025 · 6 min read

Shopify excels at e-commerce (products, cart, checkout, payments). Webflow excels at custom marketing sites and content. Using both—Webflow for brand and landing pages, Shopify for the store—is a common pattern.

When to choose Shopify alone

If you need a full online store fast and are okay with theme customization, Shopify is the default. Themes limit how far you can push design but get you live quickly.

When to add Webflow

When design and brand experience matter more than theme options, a Webflow front with Shopify backend (Buy Button, custom integrations, or headless) gives you a unique storefront with Shopify handling commerce.

Summary

Shopify for commerce; Webflow for custom design and content. Combine them when you want both.
