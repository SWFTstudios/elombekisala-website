Automation

Make, Zapier, and AI workflows that connect your site, CRM, email, and ads. Lead capture → follow-up → reporting so you spend less time on manual steps.

Example automations

1. Lead capture → CRM → email

Form or landing page submission triggers a new contact in your CRM and sends a personalized follow-up email (or sequence).

2. Inbound leads → Slack/email

New leads from Webflow, Typeform, or another source post to Slack and/or send an email so your team can respond fast.

3. Reporting and dashboards

Pull data from Google Analytics, ads, or CRM into a simple report or spreadsheet on a schedule.
