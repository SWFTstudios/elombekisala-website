Shopify + Webflow

When you want a custom-designed storefront (Webflow) with Shopify handling products, cart, and payments. Best for brands that care deeply about design and UX while needing robust e‑commerce.

Who it's for

Brands that have (or plan) a Shopify store but want a unique front-end instead of a standard theme. We connect your Webflow design to Shopify via Buy Button, custom integrations, or headless approaches depending on scope.

Typical stack

Webflow for marketing pages and custom layouts; Shopify for product catalog, cart, checkout, and fulfillment. Optional: Make or Zapier for inventory and CRM sync.
