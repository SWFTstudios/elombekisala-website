Webflow

Blurred Lines

A bold, editorial-style Webflow site for a creative brand. Strong typography and imagery-led layout.

The problem

The brand wanted a site that felt distinct and editorial, not template-driven. They needed to showcase work and attract new clients without sacrificing performance or ease of updates.

Approach

We focused on typography, spacing, and a grid that could handle mixed media. Webflow allowed custom design while keeping the CMS flexible for the team.

Build

Custom Webflow build with a portfolio CMS, animated sections where it added value, and mobile-first responsive design. No bloat—fast load and clear structure.

Results

A site that stands out visually and performs well. The client can add new projects and update copy without developer help.
