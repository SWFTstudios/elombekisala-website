Webflow · Automation

Built By Me EZ

Webflow marketing site plus automation: lead capture, CRM sync, and follow-up so the team could focus on sales.

The problem

They had a product and an audience but were manually handling leads from the site. They needed a new site and a way to get leads into their CRM and trigger follow-up without manual steps.

Approach

Design and build the site in Webflow, then connect forms to Make (and optionally Zapier) so every submission created a CRM contact and triggered an email sequence.

Build

Webflow site with clear CTAs and forms. Make.com scenarios: form submit → CRM create/update, then add to email automation. No code on the front end; all logic in the automation tool.

Results

Leads now flow automatically into their pipeline. Less manual data entry, faster response time, and a single source of truth for contacts.

“The site looks great and the automation just works. We close more leads now.”

— Client, Built By Me EZ
