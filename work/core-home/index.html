Core Home

A custom Webflow site for a home and lifestyle brand. Clean layout, strong imagery, and easy content updates.

The problem

The client needed a new marketing site that reflected their brand and could be updated in-house without code. Their previous site was outdated and hard to maintain.

Approach

We aligned on a visual direction, then built in Webflow with a CMS for key content. Responsive from the start, with clear component structure for future edits.

Build

Custom design in Webflow, CMS collections for blog and featured products, and SEO basics (meta, headings, performance). Handoff included training on updating content.

Results

Launch on time; client can update copy and imagery without developer support. Strong Lighthouse scores and a site that scales with their roadmap.

“Elombe delivered exactly what we needed—on time and easy for our team to manage.”

— Client, Core Home
